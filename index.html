Privacy Policy for OneOnOneTracker

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

OneOnOneTracker ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide the features of OneOnOneTracker, we access and collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Identification Information: When you use Google Sign-In, we access your name and email address to identify your backup profile.
  • Meeting Data: Information you manually enter, including names of people, team/role details, meeting summaries, and follow-up notes.
  • Authentication Data: Tokens provided by Google to manage your session and access authorized cloud services (Google Drive).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To organize and display your 1-on-1 meeting history and people profiles.
  • Backup and Sync: To allow you to securely back up your local database to your personal Google Drive account and restore it when needed.
  • Personalization: To display your name within the App’s settings or profile section.

3. Google Drive & API Disclosure (Limited Use)

OneOnOneTracker uses Google APIs to provide cloud backup functionality.

  • Access: The App requests access to your Google Drive (specifically the drive.appdata scope) to create and read a private backup file.
  • Limited Use: OneOnOneTracker's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
  • Privacy: We do not see, read, or share your Google Drive files. The App only interacts with the specific backup file it creates.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: By default, all meeting notes and person profiles are stored locally on your device using an encrypted database.
  • Cloud Security: When you initiate a backup, data is transmitted directly from your device to Google’s secure servers via encrypted protocols (HTTPS/TLS).
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data, meeting notes, or Google account information with any third-party companies or advertisers.

5. In-App Purchases

OneOnOneTracker offers a "Pro" version via Google Play Billing.

  • We do not collect or store your credit card or financial details. All transactions are handled securely by the Google Play Store.
  • We only receive a confirmation token from Google to verify your subscription or lifetime purchase status.

6. Data Deletion and Retention

  • On-Device Deletion: You can delete individual meetings or people, or clear all app data via your device settings at any time.
  • Cloud Deletion: You can delete your backup file directly from your Google Drive account or by contacting us for assistance.
  • Account Disconnection: You can revoke the App's access to your Google Account at any time through your Google Security Settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

OneOnOneTracker is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
